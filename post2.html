🚀 Learning to Code 🚀

Chaos, coffee, and curly braces {} 😅

Here is the truth: coding is like dating. At first, everything is confusing, you overthink every move, and Google becomes your therapist 😊.

Some days I feel like a genius, other days I forget a semicolon and my whole site collapses 💀. But that is the fun, right? Growth, chaos, and tiny wins that feel massive.

  • ☕ Fuel: coffee (obviously)
  • 💻 Tools: VS Code, a browser, and vibes
  • 📝 Notes: Do not panic. Refresh fixes a lot.
"Code is like humor. When you have to explain it, it is bad." 😂

I am not perfect, but I am consistent. And honestly? That is how people win at this game 🏆.
