🌸 Reflections on Life 🌸

Little lessons from coding, chaos, and creativity ✨

Sometimes I get stuck in my head 🤯 between code, classes, and life being messy. But then I remind myself: I’m here, I’m creating, and that matters 💖.

I’m learning that healing and growth aren’t these big dramatic moments. They’re the small wins: making a page work, writing a paragraph, or even just getting out of bed when it’s tough.

"What they called forbidden was only freedom in disguise." — Me, romanticising life again ✨

Life’s messy. But so am I. And that’s kinda the fun of it 😅.
