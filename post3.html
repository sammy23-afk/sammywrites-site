🌸 Reflections on Life 🌸

Sometimes I get stuck in my head 🤯 between code, classes, and life is a mess. But then I remember: I am here, I am creating, and that matters 💖.

I am learning that healing and growth are not these big dramatic moments. They are small wins: making a page work, writing a paragraph, or even just getting out of bed when it is tough.

“What they called forbidden was only freedom in disguise.” — Me, romanticising life again ✨

Life is messy. But so am I. And that is kinda the fun of it 😅.
